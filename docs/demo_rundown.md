# Digital Menu Demo – End-to-End Workflow

## Objective
Deliver a fast, smooth, and persuasive in-person demo of the Digital Menu product.  
Constraints: **Max 4 minutes**, **Max 8 clicks**.  
Highlight value: fast menu digitization, customer engagement, real-time orders, and analytics insight.

---

## Demo Flow

### 1. Introduction (0:00 – 1:00)
- Approach café owner, observe busy staff, set context.
- Explain value briefly: menu goes digital, staff workload reduced, insights on popular items.
- Ask permission for a **3–4 minute demo**.

---

### 2. Menu Setup (OCR / Image Upload)
1. **Live OCR attempt (optional)**: Scan paper menu with camera.
   - Shows menu items being digitized.
2. **Edit / confirm OCR results**: Demonstrate quick corrections for any misread items.
3. **Pre-tested image fallback**: Upload a verified menu image for guaranteed instant results.
   - Messaging: can maintain a pool of images for seasonal menus or different layouts.

---

### 3. QR Code Assignment
1. **Attach menu to pre-printed QR code** for instant scanning OR
2. **Generate QR code dynamically** on device (shows flexibility).
3. **Scan QR code** to verify menu appears live on mobile.
   - Owner sees end-to-end digital transformation.

---

### 4. Customer Interaction / Orders
1. Owner or simulated customer **adds items to basket**.
2. **Send order** → order appears on dashboard.
3. **Toast notification** appears: `"New order at Table 3"`.
4. Order row highlighted in dashboard automatically.

---

### 5. Analytics Overview
1. Show **menu scans**, **item views**, and **top clicked items**.
2. Highlight **real-time updates** from customer interactions.
3. Reinforce **business benefit**: insights into what customers like, helping menu planning and stock decisions.

---

### 6. Optional Notes / Messaging
- Payment flow **skipped or simulated**: focus is on menu, orders, and analytics.
- Demo emphasizes **smooth, fast, intuitive workflow**.
- Key WOW moments:  
  - OCR or image upload → instant menu display  
  - Live QR scan → menu on phone  
  - Orders appear instantly with toast notification  
  - Real-time analytics update  

---

## Summary Metrics for Demo
- **Total duration:** ≤ 4 minutes
- **Total clicks/interactions:** ≤ 8
- **Core wow moments:**  
  1. Menu digitized instantly from paper/image  
  2. QR code scan shows live menu  
  3. Order sent → dashboard updates instantly with notification  
  4. Analytics reflect user interactions  

---

## Design Considerations
- Use **pre-tested OCR images** to guarantee success in front of owner.
- **Pre-printed QR codes** for immediate scan during demo.
- **Subtle toast notifications** + auto-refresh for real-time order visibility.
- Keep demo **clean, intuitive, and fast** — avoid overwhelming the owner with too many features.
